About Us


About us

The Family Car Dealership is a collection of new and used car dealerships in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, that proudly serve the average car users and enthusiasts alike. Our family owned and operated business was opened in 2005 by Dominic Torreto and we're eager to continue our tradition of providing quality, well-maintained pre-owned vehicle options to shoppers in the area. With rugged trucks, family-friendly sedans and off-roading SUVs to explore, there'a competitively priced ride for every driver here!

Our fully stocked inventory of new and pre-owned vehicles means that drivers can explore an array of options before making any decisions as to what suits their needs. We carry models from a range of reputable brands.

When we're not assisting drivers with their auto needs, we're doing what we can to support the local community, like donating to charities in the area. We're here to help with all the needs of Addis Ababa and Ethiopian residents, so get in touch with Family Car Dealership today!
